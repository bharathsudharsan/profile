I am a Senior AI ML Software Engineer at General Motors (GM), co-leading and contributing to the research and development of GM products.

I did my PhD at the Data Science Institute, NUI Galway with funding from CONFIRM SFI Research Centre for Smart Manufacturing. I was advised by Prof. John G Breslin and Dr. Muhammad Intizar Ali.

My PhD viva and thesis was examined by Prof. Thomas Runkler and Prof. Edward Curry. My PhD research was to:

  • Design algorithms to improve the Resilience, Interoperability, Scalability (RIS) of IoT devices.
  • Deep optimization, deployment, and efficient execution of a range of ML models on AIoT boards, small CPUs, and MCUs based devices.
  • Design resource-friendly algorithms to enable tiny devices to locally re-train themselves on-the-fly using unseen real-world data.
  • Design compression algorithms to improve communication efficiency when distributed ML model training and when collaborative learning in IoT.

During PhD, I contributed to science by publishing 15+ first-author full papers, 10+ demos/short-papers at top-tier journals and conferences.

I obtained my Masters from NUI Galway in Electronics and Computer Engineering. My Master's project was supervised by Prof. Peter Corcoran.
