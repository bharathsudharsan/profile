    - Concept of adjusting traffic tiers by balancing short-term trends with long-term stability, mitigating transient fluctuations. The algorithm ensures stability during data gaps, preventing downstream computational bottlenecks.

    - Distribute vehicles to shards in a greedy manner, then refine distribution via backtracking. This refinement is flexible, making it ideal for dynamic real-time environments.
  • 2024: GM Trade Secret P108914. Geospatial Actor-based Platform for Realtime Petabyte-Scale Incremental Machine Learning as a Service.
    - Productionized actor system for resilient incremental learning by handling petabyte-scale streams with minimal cluster resource usage.
    - Our system maintains real-time insights for the entire USA map (scalable to planetary coverage) at 13 mil road-segment level by associating physical geohashes with gRPC/ML actors and orchestrating them efficiently within worker pods.
    - Our system distributes road-segment-wise data to ML actors via gRPC actors, ensuring robust data/model parallelism where each actor trains/infers on specific road segments.

    - System productionized. Generate TSS to convolve and identify spatial patterns within temporal sequences, enabling decipher complex and noisy traffic flows - achieve unparalleled forecasting accuracy in traffic analysis.
    - For congestion prediction over complex datasets with multi-seasonality, mixed trends, and autocorrelated residuals, TSS outperformed state-of-the-art Neural Prophet model in terms of execution time, ability to learn incrementally, lowest error rates across tens of thousands of roads and millions of samples.

  • 2024: Lead Inventor, GM Trade Secret P107304. Granular Geopoint-Level Map Enhancement using Multi-Dimensional Spatial Indexing.
